Gemini

  • Geminis are born between May 21st - June 20th.
  • Their zodiac symbol is twins.
  • Their element is air.
  • Their ruling planet is Mercury and their ruling house is 3rd.
  • Geminis have positive polarity.
  • Their power colours are yellow and blue.
  • Their key traits are: adaptable, social and restless.
  • They desire: success, recognition and esteem.
  • They are the most compatible with: Aries, Leo, Libra and Aquarius.
  • Gemini mantra is: "I think"
