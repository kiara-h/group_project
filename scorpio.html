Scorpio

  • Scorpios are born between October 21-November 20.
  • Their zodiac symbol is a scorpion.
  • The Scorpio element is water.
  • Their ruling planet is Mars and Pluto and their ruling house is 8th.
  • Their polarity is negative.
  • Scorpio's power colours are: black and deep red.
  • Their key traits are: intense, magnetic and controlling.
  • Scorpio's desire honesty and deep emotional connection.
  • They are most compatible with: Cancer, Virgo, Capricorn and Pisces.
  • Their mantra is: "I desire".
